# SCpay

Online payment has become integral part of once life even in tier 2&3 cities and ShareChat/Moj are well trusted & used in all these cities.
Our idea is to simply provide payment option in ShareChat/Moj app. It can also be integrated with payment for creators. We can give SC coins as a reward for successful payments to promote its usage.
Initially we will use 3rd party payment system. Later on we can build our own payment system, if it gains popularity.
